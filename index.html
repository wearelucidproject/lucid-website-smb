Do I need to already have a domain name? +
No — but you will need one before we can go live. If you don't have one yet, we can point you in the right direction. If you already have a domain (on GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.), we'll handle the DNS setup as part of your project.
What do I need to provide to get started? +
You'll fill out an onboarding form with your business info, goals, brand assets (logo, colors if you have them), and any content you want to include. Don't have everything polished? No problem — we'll work with what you have and fill in the gaps.
What's included in the 7-day delivery? +
Design, development, copywriting, one round of revisions, DNS setup, and launch. The 7-day clock starts the next business day after your completed onboarding form is submitted.
Can I edit the site myself after it's live? +
We can discuss CMS integration if self-editing is a priority for you. For most clients, ongoing updates are handled through a Lucid Project retainer — which keeps your site maintained, optimized, and evolving without the hassle of managing it yourself.
What happens after the site is live? +
Your site is yours. If you want ongoing creative direction, content, SEO updates, or marketing support, we offer retainer packages starting at $1,250/month — designed to keep growing what we built together.
How is this different from using Squarespace or Wix myself? +
DIY builders produce generic, template-looking sites that rarely convert well and score poorly on SEO. We build custom, performance-optimized sites designed around your specific business goals — not a template. The difference is visible immediately.
